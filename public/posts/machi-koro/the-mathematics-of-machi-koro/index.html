The Mathematics of "Machi Koro"

  • Nov 11, 2025

Machi Koro is a strategy (and luck) board game where players try to gain assets and money.

In each turn; each player tosses a cube (or two, if permitted) and gains (or losses) money depending on the outcome of the cube and his current assets.
